Determine whether an integer is a palindrome. 
A palindrome is an integer that reads the same both in forward order (from left to right) 
and in reverse order (from right to left).

Example1:
input: 121
output: true

Example2:
input: -121
output: false
Explain: 从左向右读，为-121。从右向左读，为121-。因此它不是一个回文数。

Example3:
input: 10
output: false
Explain: 从左向右读，为01。因此不是一个回文数
